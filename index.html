Educational Struggles in B.Tech

Most engineering students face a "Double Burnout":

Academic Problems

Drowning in complex lectures, unstructured documentation, and fear of asking seniors for help.

  • Let’s be real—staying 100% focused during long lectures is impossible. Students zone out and miss key points.
  • Most students struggle to find a clear roadmap, ending up confused by the endless tutorials online.
  • Freshers are often too intimidated to approach seniors. They desperately need advice but fear judgment.

Lifestyle Problems

Zero confidence in interviews due to lack of grooming, poor posture, and health neglect.

  • The Grooming Gap: Many students feel like they're wearing a "costume" in interviews, leading to low self-assurance.
  • The Coder’s Posture: "Tech Neck" and closed-off body language signal low energy to recruiters.
  • Health Neglect: Ignoring health isn't just about looks—it’s about the mental edge required for confidence.

How GlowStack Fixes It

We use Open Innovation to bridge the gap between your GPA and your Personality.

For Academics

  • AI Lecture Notes: Turn boring audio into summaries.
  • Doc Simplifier: Understand complex code docs.
  • Senior Bridge: An anonymous space to ask seniors.

For Lifestyle

  • Style Scan: AI recommendations for hairstyles & specs.
  • Glow Check: Instant acne detection & remedies.
  • Confidence Coach: Tips to improve posture & presence.
